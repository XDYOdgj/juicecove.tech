Midanya Farmhouse Faucet

Enhance your bathroom with the Midanya farmhouse faucet, designed to merge timeless elegance with modern efficiency. Made from premium SUS304 stainless steel, this faucet guarantees long-lasting performance and durability, making it a reliable choice for your home or any outdoor setup.

The single handle design simplifies temperature and flow control with smooth, precise adjustments. Its advanced ARC spout creates a steady, laminar water stream, offering both functionality and a refined aesthetic that complements both contemporary and vintage bathroom styles.

Available in brushed nickel or matte black finishes, this faucet adapts effortlessly to your decor. The brushed nickel option brings a polished and sophisticated look, while the matte black finish adds a bold, industrial edge that makes a statement.

Perfect for bathroom sinks, small vanities, and even RV applications, the Midanya farmhouse faucet is built for versatility. It includes all necessary mounting hardware and a pop-up drain, ensuring a seamless installation without the need for extra accessories.
