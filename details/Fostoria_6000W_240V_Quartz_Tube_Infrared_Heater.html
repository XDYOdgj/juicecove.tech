Fostoria 6000W Infrared Quartz Tube Heater

Experience the power of direct radiant heating with the Fostoria infrared heater. This efficient heating solution warms people and objects without producing noise or blowing air, making it perfect for creating a cozy and comfortable environment in workshops, garages, patios, and other hard-to-reach areas.

Featuring a dual quartz tube system and advanced reflectors, the heater maximizes warmth through conduction, convection, and radiation, ensuring optimal heat transfer. Its 6000W power output and 240V voltage deliver strong heating performance, ideal for both indoor and outdoor use.

Constructed with durable steel housing and a textured high-temp brown finish, the heater is built to last. Its corrosion-resistant components make it suitable for challenging environments such as industrial, commercial, and residential spaces. The surface mounting brackets allow for adjustable tilt up to 45 degrees, enabling targeted heating for maximum comfort and efficiency.

Fostoria's infrared quartz tube heater is not only reliable but also safe, with no moving parts to worry about. ETL Listed for use in the USA and Canada, it provides a dependable heating solution that keeps you warm without the hassle of complicated installation or maintenance.
