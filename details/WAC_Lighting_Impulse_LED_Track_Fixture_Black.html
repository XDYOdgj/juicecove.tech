WAC Lighting Impulse LED Track Fixture

Elevate your space with this modern and energy-efficient LED track light. Combining style with advanced technology, the Impulse fixture delivers superior performance through its durable and heat-dissipating die-cast aluminum body. Designed for longevity, it offers a rated life of 50,000 hours, ensuring reliable lighting for years to come.

The Impulse LED track fixture features 360-degree horizontal rotation and 180-degree vertical aiming, giving you full control over light direction. This flexibility is perfect for accentuating artwork, creating ambiance, or highlighting key areas in your home or business. It also supports smooth dimming down to 10%, allowing you to tailor brightness to any mood or occasion.

With its low-voltage design, the Impulse is compatible with 120V track systems, making installation effortless. It's available in H, J/J2, and L track configurations, so you can choose the perfect fit for your ceiling layout. The replaceable LED module ensures easy maintenance and customization, offering a wide range of color temperatures and CRI ratings to match your decor perfectly.

Title 24 compliant and designed with a 90CRI version, this fixture ensures bright, natural illumination that enhances your space without compromising on energy efficiency or style. Whether you're lighting a living room, kitchen, or commercial setting, the Impulse LED track light is the ideal choice for modern, functional, and aesthetically pleasing lighting solutions.
