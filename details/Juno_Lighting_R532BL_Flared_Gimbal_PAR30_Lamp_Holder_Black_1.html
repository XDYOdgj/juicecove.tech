Juno Lighting R532BL Flared Gimbal PAR30 Lamp Holder

Elevate your lighting setup with this premium track lighting holder. Engineered for both residential and commercial spaces, it combines functionality with elegant design for a modern, stylish solution.

This holder features a 90-degree vertical aiming system that allows precise control of light direction, making it ideal for creating the perfect ambiance in any environment. With its 358-degree rotation, you can easily adjust the angle of your PAR30 lamps, ensuring even lighting distribution across your space.

Its medium base porcelain socket is built for a secure and long-lasting fit, while the nickel-plated copper screw shell adds durability and a refined look. The copper alloy contacts ensure smooth, reliable operation and eliminate arcing, providing excellent performance with two-circuit applications.

Constructed with a drawn steel housing and a stamped gimbal ring, this lamp holder allows effortless swiveling and tilting, making installation and use simple. Whether you're replacing old lighting or creating new setups, this holder is a reliable choice that delivers exceptional value for your project.

  • Nickel Plated Copper Screw Shell: Ensures a polished, durable finish for long-lasting use.
  • Copper Alloy Contacts: Provide precise spring action for reliable, arc-free operation.
  • Drawn Steel Housing with Stamped Gimbal Ring: Offers smooth swiveling and a sturdy, elegant build.
